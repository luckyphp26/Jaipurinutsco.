CASHWES

Cashews are buttery, mildly sweet nuts loved for their creamy texture and rich taste. High in protein, iron, zinc, and healthy monounsaturated fats, cashews promote good bone health and immunity. They’re versatile—great as a snack, in gravies, or even processed into dairy alternatives like cashew milk and cheese.

ALMONDS

Almonds are crunchy, nutrient-rich nuts known for their exceptional health benefits. Packed with vitamin E, magnesium, healthy fats, and antioxidants, they support heart health, improve skin texture, and boost energy levels. Whether eaten raw, roasted, or added to desserts and breakfast bowls, almonds are a perfect everyday snack for all age groups.

Last updated 3 mins ago

PISTAS

Pistachios are vibrant green nuts with a distinctive flavor, known for being rich in fiber, protein, and healthy fats. They are a good source of antioxidants and potassium, contributing to heart health and weight management. Enjoy them roasted, salted, or in various culinary delights from desserts to main courses.
